Lasagna

Description

Layers of pasta, tomato sauce, ricotta, and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta
  • Mozzarella

Steps

  1. Cook pasta.
  2. Layer pasta, sauce, and ricotta in baking dish.
  3. Top with mozzarella.
  4. Bake for 30 mins at 350 degrees fahrenheit.
